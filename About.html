WHO WE ARE ?

TILI FAMILY

Established in 2018 as a family run business, TILI STAR TRADING L.L.C has rapidly ascended to become a premier distributor in the UAE market. In less than five years, we've meticulously curated a portfolio of over 400 items from more than 10 internationally renowned brands. Alongside our commitment to offering top-tier products, we proudly introduce our own chocolate and nuts brand, Kuche, adding a distinctive touch to our offerings.Our success is underpinned by exclusive partnerships granting us access to A+ markets, petrol stations, and online stores across the UAE. This strategic positioning has enabled us to swiftly penetrate diverse market segments and establish ourselves as a trusted name in distribution. With an unwavering focus on quality, diversity, and customer satisfaction, TILI STAR TRADING L.L.C continues to set new standards of excellence in the industry, poised for further growth and expansion in the years to come.
