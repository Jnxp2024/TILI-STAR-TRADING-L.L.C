About Takis

Takis: Bold, fiery, and always satisfying!

Takis are a popular brand of spicy rolled corn tortilla chips produced by Barcel, a division of Grupo Bimbo, a Mexican multinational bakery product manufacturing company. These snacks are known for their intense flavors and vibrant colors, and they come in various flavors, such as Fuego (hot chili pepper and lime), Nitro (habanero and lime), Crunchy Fajita, and more. Takis have gained a significant following, particularly among teenagers and young adults, who enjoy their bold taste and crunchy texture. They've become a staple snack in many households and are often found in convenience stores and supermarkets worldwide.
